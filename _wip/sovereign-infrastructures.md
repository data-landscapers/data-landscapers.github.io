  <h2>Overview</h2>
  <p>Ongoing, ad-hoc research, in this field is aimed at understanding what the long-term impact of foreign ownership of ICT infrastructures will have on African digital sovereignty.</p>
  <p>Research focuses on the undersea cable network, developments in satellite connectivity and data centre construction. The focus is on finance, ownership and influence.</p>
